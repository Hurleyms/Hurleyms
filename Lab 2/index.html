Indianapolis

Location

Indianapolis is located in the central part of the state of Indiana. It exists in Marion County. The White River runs through the city.

About

History

Indianapolis was founded in 1821. Indianapolis's layout was designed by Alexander Ralston and Elias Pym Fordham. It became a hub for trains and is the reason why Indiana is called the "Crossroads of America".

Landmarks

Indianapolis has many Landmarks. A first major landmark is Monument Circle and the State Soliders and Sailors Monument. We also have the Benjamin Harrison Presidential Site, the Eiteljorg Museum of American Indians and Western Art, the Indianapolis Motor Speedway, and Lucas Oil Stadium.

Community

Indianapolis has a thriving community of interesting locations, experiences, night life, theatres, and many more. You'll certainly be able to find at least something to keep you occupied during any free time you may have.
