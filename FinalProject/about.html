About Me

My name is Matthew Hurley. I am aspiring to work in a Media Arts and Science field. My current focus is Digital Storytelling, but that may change as I aquire more skills and figure out my path in life. I have worked with a number of digital tools throughout my life, some of which I consider myself to be pretty good at and others I would say I am just okay at.

The first tool I will mention is Photoshop. While I certainly wouldn't call myself a Photoshop wizard, I will say it is one of my most used tools. I have made logos, edited game textures, and a bunch more. The second tool I will discuss is DaVinci Resolve. I have been editing videos for several years at this point and would say Resolve is likely the tool I am best with, and while that skill level is still not comparable to experts in the field, I would still say it is one of my best aspects. The third and final tool I will focus on is Visual Studio Code. I made this site with with VSC and I would say it is the one I have the least experience with, but I can still use the program and am improving all the time.

I also have other non-digital skills as well. I have the ability to work as part of a team because of my experience as a lifeguard, and I have the ability to work alone from my experience as a solo tennis instructor. I am also a bartender currently so I have experience working directly with customers.
